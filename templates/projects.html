  • Snake AI

    You can launch the web version of 'Snake AI' by clicking on the 'Play Snake AI' link. When you do so, you will first be prompted to enter two parameters associated with the snake's distance from food and the snake's head's distance from its own body, respectively. The snake will then struggle to survive until it hits itself and the game is over. At the end of a game, a number called "fitness" which is obtained based on the snake's performance will be displayed By trying different parameters, you will obtain different 'fitness' scores. try to see if you can beat the previous high scores.
